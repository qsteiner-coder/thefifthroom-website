Our Story

Built by craftsmen,
not a franchise.

The Fifth Room is a Northern Utah garage transformation company built on one principle: your garage deserves the same attention as every other room in your home. We are owner-operated — meaning Quincy is on every project, overseeing the work and accountable to you from first call to final walk-through.

Based in Kaysville and serving the entire Wasatch Front — Davis, Weber, Salt Lake, and Utah counties — we do one thing and one thing only: garages. No kitchens, no bathrooms, no additions. Just garage spaces, done with the kind of focus and expertise that comes from doing the same thing every single day.

What We Build

Every project.
One standard.

From a single-coat floor to a full garage-to-gym transformation, we bring the same level of precision and care to every job. No shortcuts. No cheap materials. Just work you'll be proud to show off.

01
Polyaspartic & Epoxy Flooring

Premium polyaspartic coatings built for Utah's freeze-thaw cycles. Fast cure times, hot-tire resistant, and UV stable — no yellowing, no peeling, no compromise.

02
Custom Storage Systems

Wall shelving, overhead racks, slatwall panels, custom cabinetry, and workbenches — designed as a complete system for how you actually use your space.

03
Insulation & Drywall

Properly insulated garages are livable year-round in Utah. We handle batt, blown-in, and spray foam — finished with professional-grade drywall and paint.

04
Gym & Sauna Buildouts

From commercial rubber flooring and mirror walls to custom cedar sauna rooms — we handle the full build. Gym conversions, sauna installs, ventilation, and dedicated electrical all under one project.

05
Garage Organization

A beautiful garage is only as good as how it functions day-to-day. We assess how you actually use your space and build an organization system around your life — tools, sports gear, seasonal storage, and everything in between.

06
Electrical & Lighting

LED shop lighting upgrades, dedicated circuits, EV charger rough-in, sub-panel installs — coordinated with licensed Utah electricians and pulled into your final project.
